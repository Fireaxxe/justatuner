Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Thank you for choosing Just A Tuner. Your privacy is paramount to us. This policy outlines how we handle any data when you use our iOS app.

1. No Data Collection

Just A Tuner works entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. How the App Works

  • All pitch detection runs locally on your device.
  • No audio or analysis is uploaded or transmitted.
  • Your tuning settings (A4 pitch, noise threshold) are stored only on your device via UserDefaults.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not integrate any analytics, advertising trackers, or external SDKs that collect user data. (AdMob only serves non-personalized banner ads; no user-level tracking.)

4. Security & Control

You remain in control. You can reset or adjust tuning settings from within the app. No history logs or audio recordings are ever stored.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. If we do, the new version will appear here with an updated “Effective Date.”
